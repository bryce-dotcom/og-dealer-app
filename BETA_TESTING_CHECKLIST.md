## Questions for Beta Testers

1. **First Impression:** What was your first reaction when you logged in?

2. **Ease of Use:** On a scale of 1-10, how easy was it to:
   - Add inventory?
   - Create a deal?
   - Generate documents?

3. **Most Valuable Feature:** What feature saves you the most time?

4. **Pain Points:** What was frustrating or confusing?

5. **Missing Features:** What do you wish it had?

6. **Pricing:** What would you expect to pay for this monthly?

7. **Would you switch?** Would you switch from your current system to OG DiX?

8. **Who else?** Who else in the dealer community should we invite?
